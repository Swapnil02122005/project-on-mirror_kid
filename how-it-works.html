How Mirrorkid Works

This guide explains how parents and children use Mirrorkid, with visual examples.

1. Register & Choose Role

Sign up as a Parent or Child. Parents monitor; children can see who is watching them.

Tip: Use your regular email address. You can change your role later.

2. Pair Devices

On the Child phone, open Home to see a code + QR. On the Parent dashboard, enter the code or scan the QR to connect.

Secure: Pairing is short-lived and device-bound. You can disconnect anytime from the parent device.

3. Monitor Activities

Parents see a timeline of activities and a usage summary (time spent per app/category). Children see exactly who is watching.

Example walk-through

  1. Parent registers and logs in as a parent.
  2. Child registers and logs in as a child; a code like 8FQ-3K2-90A and a QR appear.
  3. Parent opens Home, enters the code or scans the child’s QR.
  4. Parent sees Activity Log + Usage Analytics; child sees the parent’s name in their dashboard.
Privacy note: Data is encrypted in transit and visible only to authorized accounts. You control permissions and can disconnect anytime.

FAQs

Do I need to install anything on the child phone?
No additional installation is required for this demo. In production, a companion app would collect activity data with consent.
How do I disconnect?
Go to the Parent dashboard and click Disconnect. The child cannot disconnect.
What activity types are visible?
Screen time aggregates and high-level app/category usage. Exact data depends on permissions and platform policies.
